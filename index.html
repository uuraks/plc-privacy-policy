Privacy Policy

App: PLC Ladder Learn
Package: com.aksoy.plcladderlearn
Last updated: April 26, 2026

1. Introduction

PLC Ladder Learn ("the App") is an educational application for learning Siemens S7 PLC Ladder Diagram programming. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the App.

2. No Account Required

The App does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information such as name, email address, or phone number. All learning progress is stored locally on your device.

3. Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements to free users. AdMob may collect and use an advertising identifier (ad ID) from your device to show relevant ads. This data is handled according to Google's Privacy Policy.

Premium users who have purchased the lifetime upgrade do not see advertisements.

4. In-App Purchases

The App offers a one-time lifetime premium purchase through Google Play Billing. Purchase transactions are processed by Google and are subject to Google Payments Privacy Policy. We do not store or process any payment information on our servers.

5. Local Data Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device using device storage:

  • Lesson and exercise progress
  • Experience points (XP) and level
  • Daily streak information
  • App language preference
  • Premium purchase status

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

6. Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob – advertising (Privacy Policy)
  • Google Play Billing – in-app purchases (Privacy Policy)

7. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App does not require any personal information to function.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
